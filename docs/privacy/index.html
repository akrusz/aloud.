Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

aloud is built to be private. There's no account, no tracking, and no aloud server that receives your meditation sessions. This policy explains, plainly, what happens to your data.

The short version

  • aloud has no backend and no user accounts. We don't collect, store, or receive your content.
  • Your sessions stay on your device.
  • aloud connects to the AI and voice services you choose. When you pick a cloud service, your text (and, for some voice options, audio) is sent to that provider and handled under their privacy policy, not ours.
  • You can run aloud fully on-device for maximum privacy, if your hardware is capable.

What aloud processes, and where it goes

Your voice

Your microphone audio is used to transcribe what you say into text. Where supported, transcription runs on your device and the audio is not sent anywhere. aloud does not record or upload your audio to us.

Your words and the AI facilitator

To generate responses, aloud sends your transcribed messages and the conversation so far to the language-model provider you select in Settings:

  • On-device providers (e.g. a local model): your words never leave your device.
  • Cloud providers (e.g. Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, Venice, Groq): your text is sent to that provider's API and handled under their privacy policy and terms. aloud neither receives nor stores it.

You choose the provider and supply your own credentials, which are stored locally on your device.

Spoken responses

Facilitator responses are turned into speech. Depending on the voice you choose, this happens on your device (system voices) or via a cloud voice service, in which case the response text is sent to that service under their policy.

Saved sessions

aloud can save your session transcripts and a short summary locally on your device so you can revisit them. They stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them. There is no cloud sync.

App updates

aloud may check for new versions (a basic network request that does not include your session content).

What we don't do

  • No accounts or logins.
  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No analytics or usage tracking.
  • No selling or sharing of personal data; we don't have it.

Third-party services

When you use a cloud AI or voice provider, your use of that service is governed by its own privacy policy and terms. Please review the policy of whichever provider you connect. aloud is not responsible for how third-party providers handle data you send them through your own account or key.

Your control

  • Choose on-device providers (where available) to keep everything local.
  • Your credentials and settings live on your device. Clear them anytime.
  • Delete saved sessions from your device whenever you like.

Children's privacy

aloud is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above will change. Significant changes will be reflected here.
