two ways to practice

exploration

a dyadic format. you speak what you're experiencing in the moment. aloud. asks brief, gentle questions to help you go further in.

mix and match attention focuses — body, emotions, parts work — with vibes like playful, compassionate, spacious. dial in how directive you want the guidance, from barely-there to actively steering.

noting

you pick the participants — AIs, fixed phrases, sound effects, or no one at all. each takes a turn naming a sensation in their awareness in one or two words.

watching the cycle of rest → cue → notice → speak turns out to be a surprisingly clarifying object for attention. solo noting works too.
